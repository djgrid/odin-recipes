Chili Recipe

Chili - you know what this is

Ingredients:

  • 0.5 - 1 kg ground meat of your choice.
  • Chili Powder
  • Half a medium/large onion, diced
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1-2 cans diced tomatos, partially drained
  • Optional: Canned pinto beans and/or canned sweet corn
  • Optjonal: 1 can/bottle beer
  • Diced green chiles - you can cheat and use Ro-Tel here and use one less can diced tomatos
  • Any other spice or ingredient that you think might taste good.

Instructions:

  1. A lot of this is a "do what feels right for you" either do it or don't
  2. Brown and drain the meat. If you want you can add the beer and let the meat simmer in it for a while.
  3. Transfer the browned meat to a pot.
  4. Add the canned ingredients. Drain (or not drain) the diced tomatos to get the consistency you like
  5. Add the onions and spices.
  6. Simmer on low/medium heat for a while to let the flavors and everything combine. You can also just put it in a slow cooker and leave it for a few hours
