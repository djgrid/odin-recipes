Shortbread Recipe

Just three ingredients and about 40 minutes of time.

Ingredients:

  • 150g flour, plus a little extra for dusting.
  • 100g butter, cubed.
  • 50g fine grained sugar. Regular granulated will work, too.

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 170C/325F
  2. In a bowl, using your hands mix the flour, sugar and butter until the mixure looks like breadcrumbs, then squeeze it together as a dough.
  3. On a lightly floured surface use a rolling pin to roll out the dough to about 0.5cm/0.25 inches thick.
  4. Cut the dough into fingers and place on a lined or greased baking tray.
  5. Chill the dough in the fridge for 20 minutes, then bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Remove from oven and leave to cool for 10 minutes.
