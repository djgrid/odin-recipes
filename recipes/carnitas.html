Carnitas Recipe

Classic Mxeican Meat Dish

Ingredients:

  • 2-ish kg. pork shoulder/butt - pork for Pernil will work well too
  • Garlic, cumin, onion - to taste
  • 300-500 ml Orange Juice or Coke - Cane sugar, either Mexican Coke or Passover Coke
  • Lard - 2-3 tbsp

Instructions:

  1. Cut the pork into 4-5 cm cubes/pieces. Remove bones if possible but not necessary as the meat will come off as it cooks.
  2. Put the meat in a large pot or frying pan and add enough water to cover the meat.
  3. Add the lard, spices and Coke/OJ.
  4. Simmer until the liquid is almost gone. Skim off any foam/crud that rises to the top.
  5. Once the liquid is mostly gone increase the heat to high.
  6. Fry the meat until until it's browned and crispy. Shred the meat into smaller pices while it is frying.
